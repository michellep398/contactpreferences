Integrated Data Flow Process

1
Patient Creation: MDP/CRM creates new patient record with initial opt-in preferences and generates Lead ID, then syncs to Promptly
2
Consent Capture: Any patient touchpoint (website, portal, kiosk, staff override) captures consent changes and immediately updates Promptly
3
Real-time Sync: Promptly processes preference changes and syncs to MDP/CRM with both Promptly ID and Lead ID for complete integration
4
Marketing Control: MDP/CRM receives opt-out signals and immediately halts all automated marketing campaigns for the specific patient
5
Patient Empowerment: Patients maintain full control through Promptly portal, managing both marketing and reminder preferences with separate contact methods

Technical Integration Requirements

Promptly Database Enhancement: Add dedicated "Lead_ID" field to patient data tables enabling seamless MDP/CRM integration and bi-directional sync
MDP/CRM Database Enhancement: Implement "Promptly_ID" field in patient records for complete integration and preference tracking
API Integration Layer: Develop real-time sync capabilities between systems ensuring instant preference updates and marketing compliance
Comprehensive Audit System: Track all consent changes with detailed timestamps, source attribution, and staff member identification for compliance
Dual-Track Compliance: Maintain separate preference tracking for marketing versus reminder/recall communications with distinct contact methods and rules
